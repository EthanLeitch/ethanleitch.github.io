Tools

This is a curated list of tools I use, and I believe that everyone might find them useful

Online

  • FileBin – temporary file host
  • ImgOps – metatool for image manipulation
  • mail.tm – temporary email addresses to avoid spam

Downloadable

  • yt-dlp – download videos from basically any site, such as YouTube, Instagram, Reddit, Twitter, etc.

Browser Extensions

Using the internet in 2023 is practically impossible without these

  • uBlock Origin – the best adblocker. Others, like AdBlock, let through sponsored ads
  • SponsorBlock – skips past YouTube sponsorship segments
  • I don't care about cookies – opt-out of cookie policies
